Editorial standards

The same rules apply whether a post is sponsored or not.

  • All sponsored content is clearly labelled #ad or #sponsored per FTC guidelines. This is non-negotiable.
  • We retain full editorial control over copy and claims. No health claim appears on this page that isn't supported by published research — for any partner, at any price point.
  • Partners receive a draft for review before publishing, with a 48-hour review window. Copy is never altered to overstate what the evidence supports.
  • We don't accept partnerships from brands whose products directly contradict content we've already published. The integrity of the archive matters more than any single deal.
  • One sponsored post per week, maximum. This is a ceiling, not a floor — it protects the signal that makes this page worth partnering with in the first place.
